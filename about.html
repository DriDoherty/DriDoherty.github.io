About Me

I have always been eager to figure out how things work and how to best utilize technology to enhance our lives. I had a successful career in software engineering building enterprise software for corporate treasury management, managing development projects & teams and eventually leading the software development department of a mutilinational corporation.

I left software development to concentrate on raising my two amazing sons, who move me to look at the world with fresh eyes. Even while home, I found myself utilizing my skills to help local organizations with their technology needs, learning some VB, and basic HTML & CSS along the way.

After a second career, this time in providing home technology and automation solutions, I am eager to get back to my days of creating software. Rutgers Coding Bootcamp is my first step in realizing that goal.
